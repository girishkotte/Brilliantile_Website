Brainwave Analytics With Brilliantile

We assist in building cognitive data models for bio medical research institutes. Recently, we built data models for brain waves data comprising the cognitive descriptive, diagnostic and predictive data models which helps to predict the teenage behavior. Rather than depending on predefined rules and structured queries to uncover answers, cognitive analytics relies on systems that draw from a wide variety of potentially relevant information and connections to generate hypotheses. It expresses possible answers as recommendations, and includes the system’s own ranking of how confident it is in their accuracy. This process differs markedly from traditional analysis in that as more data is fed into a machine learning system, the system learns, which results in higher-quality insights and more accurate hypotheses.

Descriptive Analytics

Brilliantile has been brought to market with a deep initiative of précised statistics, derived from participants performance to provide accurate descriptive analytics.

Diagnostic Analytics

We have implemented the bare minimum criteria for diagnosing the brain wave data analytics, in order to provide our clients a full proof system.

Predictive Analytics

Predictive analytics based on the descriptive and diagnostic analytics empowers the further demands from brainwave analytics.
