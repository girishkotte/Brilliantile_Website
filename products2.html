Applications

The PSA process begins with clustering and segmenting of historical data. A new patient on whom the PSA process needs to be carried out is selected and his/her demographic details are recorded. The patient is categorized into one of the pre designed clusters and once his/her results are available, the results of the PSA are displayed, predicting the probable diagnosis and its probability of associated. As the patient’s clinical details are entered into the EHR, they are used in real time to fine tune the segmentation process.

It is the cerebrum of Brilliantile,using complex mathematical, statistical and cognitive algorithms to identify patterns. PSA predicts re-admission rates, disease progression,aids in risk stratification thus, decreasing re-admission penalties.

Re-admission Rate Prediction renders hospitals and healthcare systems to attain predictable re-admission, LOS, surgery prediction, Sepsis prediction, complications and disease progression. This type of prediction improves clinical outcomes, reduces ED visits and enables better utilization of physicians and resources by simplifying and optimizing workloads. The aim is to develop superior quality of care and upgrade the entire healthcare system.

List of Applications & their summary

ACO (Accountable Care Organizations)

Our ACO (Accountable Care Organizations) application helps physicians understand and manage their patient data efficiently. To aid with navigation, in the ever changing healthcare field, Brilliantile ACO module lets physicians and administrators visualize massive amounts of data on patient history and care patterns, providing them with critical data to make accurate predictions on topics such as patient re-admission. The application keeps physicians up to date with evolving regulations and standards, tracks requisite quality measures and visualizes information in a clear manner. Dashboards help private practices, large hospitals and other healthcare facility centers track their progress toward these goals. With comprehensive prediction and complex data, healthcare professionals are transformed into ‘Data Scientists’, enabling them to spend more time focusing on their patients.

GRAPH

The Graph application provides a pathway to visually explore the semantic relationships between data objects that are represented as networks of nodes and edges.
The Graph application assists in visualizing how communication, payment, shipping, and other data move through a network. It includes a timeline to visualize the sequencing of events and a time wheel to understand the periodicity and frequency of repetitive events.
Filtering tools allow users to compute interesting objects. An integrated histogram helps with selection and filtering of objects that have similar properties, like the same address, phone number, city, or domain name.
Users can adjust the layout of nodes and edges on the Graph to view different network characteristics, like hierarchical relationships. A suite of presentation tools allows analysts to annotate and enhance their graphs for use in presentations.

MAP

The Map application tenders geo spatial analytic competencies. It combines the visualization of geo-located objects on a map with histogram, timeline and time wheel visualizations. A heat map visualization illuminates the density of interesting objects on the map.
The imagery on the Map is completely pluggable, allowing users to switch between different sources of imagery, integrate private imagery, and create composite imagery to combine two or more sources of imagery.
KML and Shape files can be imported as independent map layers. Shapes contained in these layers can be used to select and filter objects that lie in a similar region (like a county, census plot, or state). Layers can be colored and labeled according to calculations performed on the data they contain.

OBJECT EXPLORER

The Object Explorer application allows users to drill down on objects of interest within extensive data sets.

BROWSER

Users can view and apply structure to unstructured documents within the Browser application. As they read raw text, users can "tag" particular words, thereby associating a document with a particular object or objects in the Dynamic Ontology and making this data available for analysis in other Brilliantile applications.

MOBILE

Brilliantile Mobile lengthens field for real-time, distributed operations that require collaboration and data collection in highly fluid situations, like post-disaster humanitarian response and coordinated law enforcement operations. Available for both Android and iOS mobile platforms, the Brilliantile Mobile client enables real-time coordination between field operators and users at base stations. Mobile users can file field reports, upload photos and videos, track the location of their teammates,search and explore data integrated into the Brilliantile platform.​
