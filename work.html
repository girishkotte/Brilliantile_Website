How Does Brilliantile Work?

Brilliantile is a modular snow removal system that operates using sensors to activate a heating element when snow accumulation reaches a certain threshold.

During winters, heavy snowfall is observed throughout the country, disrupting normal life and affecting properties. Homeowners have to take on the responsibility of removing accumulated snow from the roof, which is extremely risky, or they have to depend on professionals, which is time consuming and expensive. Our idea provides a solution through an innovative IoT modular snow removal tile, called Brilliantile, that operates using sensors to activate a heating element when snow accumulation reaches a certain threshold. Moreover, all of this can be done with just a tap of a button. Safety, cost, efficiency, and labor intensity factors aretaken into account to provide a broad understanding on the benefits of this proposed solution. Market research on several aspects of product design, pricing policy, revenue generation, SWOT (Strengths, Weakness, Opportunities, Threats) analysis, and marketing plan is also conducted. When the snowfall starts, the load sensors accurately measure the weight of the accumulating snow. The controller activates the heating element whenever the weight of snow exceeds a certain threshold. The controller also sends a notification, via a mobile application, to the user about the weight of the load and informs that the heating element has been activated. The heating element melts the snow accumulated on the tile. When the weight falls below a certain threshold, the controller deactivates the heating element and notifies the user. The mobile application is an added benefit making everything easy for the user to access. The internal threshold for activating heating element can be overridden via the app. The user can set alerts or notifications and choose to enable or disable them. Manual intervention is also possible where the user can turn on or off the heating element using mobile app. Not only that, the weather data can also be gathered and stored for future analysis and further advancements from this data can be seen in the years to follow.This solution of modular removal of the snow is easy to install and expandable over a period of time. It is a cloud-based technology and thus, can sync operation with prevailing weather conditions. It has minimal maintenance, with information and control being at the fingertips of the user. It is powered by solar energy, hence is a very cost effective solution over the long run. The tile is also operational during power failures, as it can be backed by rechargeable batteries. The most important feature is that it eliminates strenuous manual labor, due to its remote operation feature. Over a period of time, the technological benefit will include the use of machine learning logic, that will identify patterns based on large amounts of data. User experience will be seamless, easy to use and act upon, requiring minimal human intervention. There would be simplification of data access through creation of proprietary platform. Solutions would focus on preserving battery by utilizing intelligent and flexible software, and emphasis being more on security with high encryption.
